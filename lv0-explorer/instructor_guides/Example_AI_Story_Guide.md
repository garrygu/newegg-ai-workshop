# üé¨ Example AI Story: "Luna's AI Adventure"

**Purpose:** This is a complete example story you can create and show to students at the beginning of Session 1. It demonstrates all the AI tools they'll learn to use.

**Story Length:** 1-2 minutes  
**Theme:** "A Day in the Life of My AI Helper"  
**Target Audience:** Students in the Explorer Level workshop

---

## üìñ Story Overview

**Title:** "Luna's AI Adventure"  
**Concept:** A student named Luna discovers how AI can help her with a school project, leading to a creative adventure.

**Story Structure:**
- **Beginning:** Luna is stuck on a school project
- **Middle:** AI helps her brainstorm, create images, and find solutions
- **End:** Luna completes her project and shares it with the class

---

## üé® Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Problem (Beginning)
**Duration:** 15-20 seconds

**Visual:**
- Image prompt: *"A young student sitting at a desk with a laptop, looking confused and surrounded by books and papers, warm morning sunlight through window, cartoon style, friendly and approachable"*

**Narration Script:**
> "Meet Luna. It's Monday morning, and she has a big science project due on Friday. She's stuck. How can she make her project about space exploration interesting and creative?"

**Visual Notes:** Show Luna looking thoughtful, maybe with a blank document on her laptop.

---

### Scene 2: Discovering AI Help (Middle - Part 1)
**Duration:** 20-25 seconds

**Visual:**
- Image prompt: *"A friendly AI assistant character appearing on a computer screen, glowing with soft blue light, helping a student, modern and friendly design, cartoon style"*

**Narration Script:**
> "Luna remembers her teacher mentioned AI tools. She opens ChatGPT and asks: 'Help me brainstorm ideas for a space exploration project.' The AI suggests creating a story about a journey to Mars, with images and a presentation!"

**Visual Notes:** Show the AI assistant interface or a representation of ChatGPT helping.

---

### Scene 3: Creating with AI (Middle - Part 2)
**Duration:** 25-30 seconds

**Visual:**
- Image prompt: *"A colorful illustration of a rocket ship flying through space with planets in the background, vibrant colors, cartoon style, exciting and adventurous"*
- **Video:** Animate the rocket moving through space (if possible)

**Narration Script:**
> "Luna uses DALL¬∑E to create images for her story. She types: 'A rocket ship flying through space with colorful planets, cartoon style.' The AI creates amazing artwork! She creates more images: an astronaut on Mars, a space station, and a beautiful nebula."

**Visual Notes:** Show multiple AI-generated images appearing one by one, or a montage of space-themed images.

---

### Scene 4: Putting It Together (Middle - Part 3)
**Duration:** 20-25 seconds

**Visual:**
- Image prompt: *"A student presenting a colorful presentation on a screen showing space images, classmates watching, classroom setting, happy and successful atmosphere, cartoon style"*

**Narration Script:**
> "Luna combines her AI-generated images into a slideshow. She uses AI voice tools to add narration. Her project tells the story of a journey to Mars, complete with beautiful images and a clear voice explaining each step."

**Visual Notes:** Show Luna's completed presentation or her working on assembling the pieces.

---

### Scene 5: Success! (End)
**Duration:** 15-20 seconds

**Visual:**
- Image prompt: *"A student holding up a completed project with a big smile, colorful space-themed artwork visible, teacher and classmates clapping, celebration atmosphere, cartoon style"*

**Narration Script:**
> "On Friday, Luna presents her project. Her teacher is impressed! Luna learned that AI isn't just a tool‚Äîit's a creative partner that helps bring ideas to life. And that's just the beginning of Luna's AI adventure!"

**Visual Notes:** Show Luna happy and successful, maybe with her project displayed.

---

### Step 2: Create Voice Narration (Using AI Voice Tools)

**Full Narration Script** (read this into your voice tool, or have AI read it):

```
Meet Luna. It's Monday morning, and she has a big science project due on Friday. She's stuck. How can she make her project about space exploration interesting and creative?

Luna remembers her teacher mentioned AI tools. She opens ChatGPT and asks: Help me brainstorm ideas for a space exploration project. The AI suggests creating a story about a journey to Mars, with images and a presentation!

Luna uses DALL¬∑E to create images for her story. She types: A rocket ship flying through space with colorful planets, cartoon style. The AI creates amazing artwork! She creates more images: an astronaut on Mars, a space station, and a beautiful nebula.

Luna combines her AI-generated images into a slideshow. She uses AI voice tools to add narration. Her project tells the story of a journey to Mars, complete with beautiful images and a clear voice explaining each step.

On Friday, Luna presents her project. Her teacher is impressed! Luna learned that AI isn't just a tool‚Äîit's a creative partner that helps bring ideas to life. And that's just the beginning of Luna's AI adventure!
```

**Tools to Use:**
- ElevenLabs: https://elevenlabs.io/ (free tier available)
- Google Text-to-Speech
- Microsoft Azure Speech Services
- Or record your own voice!

**Voice Settings:**
- Choose a friendly, clear voice (young adult or teenager voice works well)
- Pace: Moderate (not too fast)
- Tone: Enthusiastic but natural

---

### Step 3: Create Video/Animation (Optional but Recommended)

**Option A: Simple Slideshow**
- Use PowerPoint, Google Slides, or Canva
- Add each image as a slide
- Set transitions between slides (3-4 seconds per slide)
- Add the voice narration as background audio

**Option B: Animated Video**
- Use RunwayML or similar tool to animate Scene 3 (the rocket)
- Or use simple animation tools like Animaker, Powtoon, or Canva Video

**Option C: Video Editing**
- Use iMovie, Windows Movie Maker, or online tools like CapCut
- Import all images
- Add voice narration
- Add simple transitions and background music (optional)

---

### Step 4: Assemble Everything

**Recommended Tools:**
1. **Canva** (Easiest for beginners)
   - Create a video project
   - Upload images
   - Add voice narration
   - Add simple transitions
   - Export as video

2. **PowerPoint/Google Slides**
   - Create slideshow with images
   - Record narration or add audio file
   - Export as video

3. **Simple Video Editor**
   - Import images in order
   - Add voice track
   - Export as MP4

**Final Video Specifications:**
- **Format:** MP4
- **Duration:** 1-2 minutes
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920x1080) or 720p minimum
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (standard video format)
